Anticipated Effects

This essay does not argue that the global viewers had the fact that Psy is Asian in mind and intentionally decided that Gangnam Style was because Asians are supposed to be hilarious. Rather, it argues that from a long, extensive exposure to stereotypical images of Asians on mass media formed the viewers’ subliminal tastes so they would prefer Asians to be funny than sexy. By introducing more hot Asian stars on screen through ‘cultural affirmative action’, viewers will gradually grow a fairer taste towards celebrities of diverse races.
