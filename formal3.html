Background Information

Modern scientific researches are very sophisticated and quite often require the use of expensive resources including research workforce, state-of-the-art experiment devices, and powerful analyzing computing systems. Naturally, one researcher can rarely fund his or her project on her own; most of the times, a researcher must compile and submit a document briefly describing the purpose and the goal of the research, called a research proposal, to a funding authority and have it approved in order to receive funds for the project.
